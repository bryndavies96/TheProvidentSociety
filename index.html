Who are we?

We are Edenfield’s biggest & most versatile events venue. Built in the 1860s, this former cooperative warehouse has been sympathetically and lovingly restored so that it remains a symbol of Lancashire's industrial heritage. We are fully equipped with a top-quality in-house lighting and sound system. The Grand Hall can be tailored exactly to your theme and offers top quality on-site catering by The Drop Off and a fully licensed bar for your guests. If you’re looking for a party space with a bit of oomph this one is definitely for you. The Provident Society is a canvas of unlimited potential and can cater for both corporate and creative requirements.
